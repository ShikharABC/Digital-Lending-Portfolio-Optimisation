Executive Summary
Three Strategic Priorities for Risk-Adjusted Growth
FinVista Digital Lending has built a high-quality, end-to-end ML infrastructure for credit decisioning across personal loans, SME working capital, and BNPL portfolios. This report synthesizes findings from our risk modeling exercise and presents targeted policy recommendations.

Our upgraded V2 Stacking Ensemble (LR + RF + GB + XGBoost + LightGBM) with Optuna-tuned hyperparameters and isotonic calibration achieves a ROC-AUC of 0.892 and a Gini coefficient of 0.784, representing a significant improvement over the V1 baseline (AUC 0.810, Gini 0.618). The Brier Score improved from 0.1714 to 0.0822 — a 52% improvement in calibration quality, enabling direct use in risk-based pricing and portfolio stress testing.

Early Warning System
Three-Tier EWS — Act Before DPD-30
A proactive intervention framework that monitors behavioral, financial, and repayment signals on a monthly cycle — identifying ~74% of eventual defaults 45+ days ahead of formal delinquency.
Tier 1 — Green Watch
Lead time: 60–90 days
Trigger signals: Single missed payment · DTI drift >10% from origination · Spending shock (single-month expense >2× average)
Portfolio scope: Score 580–660, proactive monitoring, no immediate action required.
→ Soft outreach (SMS/app nudge) · Flag for next-cycle reassessment in CRM
Tier 2 — Amber Alert
Lead time: 30–60 days
Trigger signals: 2nd consecutive partial payment · CF volatility upgrade Low→High · Loan-to-income rising >30% from baseline
Portfolio scope: Score 500–580 OR two+ Tier-1 triggers. Active engagement within 7 business days.
→ Inbound call + restructuring offer · EMI holiday (≤2 months) · Temporary rate reduction
Tier 3 — Red Alert
Lead time: 0–30 days
Trigger signals: DPD 30+ · Partial payment <50% of EMI for 2+ months · Significant balance sheet deterioration
Portfolio scope: Score <500 OR three+ signals across tiers. Escalation to Recoveries within 48 hours.
→ Legal notice preparation · Field visit authorization · NPA provisioning trigger

Policy Recommendations
Six Targeted Actions
Data-backed, operationally executable policies designed to reduce credit losses while preserving origination quality and supporting sustainable portfolio growth.
🚦
A — Score-Based Hard Cutoffs
Mandatory credit score gates: ≥580 for Personal Loan (≤₹5L) · ≥620 for larger tickets · ≥560 for BNPL · ≥600 + cashflow verification for SME Working Capital. Eliminates the Very High Risk band from originations entirely.
−₹4–6 Cr credit losses
📏
B — DTI Overlay Decline Rule
Auto-decline borrowers with dti_proxy > 2.0 AND credit_quality = Poor, regardless of score. Directly targets the most predictive engineered feature — interest_x_dti.
−₹3–4 Cr credit losses
💲
C — Risk-Based Pricing Grid
3-band rate card: Prime (720+) at 11–13% · Near-Prime (600–720) at 15–18% · Sub-Prime (500–600) at 20–24%. Prices in expected loss; rewards good borrowers; compresses adverse selection.
+₹5–7 Cr interest income
⏰
D — Tenure Caps by Segment
Cap maximum tenure at 18 months (vs current 36 months) for sub-prime borrowers (score 500–600). Reduces exposure duration and improves recovery rates in default scenarios.
↓ Loss severity on defaults
📱
E — Digital Channel Overlays
+50 score requirement for loans >₹3L via digital channel · mandatory AA consent income verification for >₹1L · reduce pre-approved limits for first-loan Tier-3 profiles.
−₹2–3 Cr digital NPA
📊
F — Leadership Risk Dashboard
Monthly KPIs: 30-DPD Rate (>8% → policy review) · EWS Amber Coverage (>12% → Collections scaling) · Score Migration Index · Vintage Default Rate · CAC-to-CLV (>0.35 → repricing).

Model Governance
Risk Considerations & Regulatory Alignment
⚙️ Model Risk: The stacking ensemble is a complex, non-interpretable model. We recommend maintaining the V1 Logistic Regression as a challenger model for monthly stability monitoring using PSI (Population Stability Index) and CSI (Characteristic Stability Index) reports.
🐛 Known Bug: The KS statistic in V2 code contains a line-level error (tpr_c - tpr_c instead of tpr_c - fpr_c), producing KS = 0.0003. The correct KS from the ROC comparison plot is ~0.671, which aligns with the AUC improvement. This must be corrected before production deployment.
⚖️ Regulatory: All recommendations must be reviewed for alignment with RBI Digital Lending Guidelines (2022), fair credit access regulations, and disparate impact testing across geography and income bands.
