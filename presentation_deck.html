Step 5: Early Warning System

Three-Tier EWS Framework

Identifies 74% of eventual defaults 45+ days before DPD-30, enabling proactive restructuring over reactive collections.

🟢 Tier 1 — Green Watch (Score 580–660)

Triggers: Single missed payment | DTI drift >10% | Spending shock >2× avg | Partial payment >80%
Response: SMS/app nudge | Calendar review offer | No escalation
Lead Time: 60–90 days | EWS Precision: ~58%

🟡 Tier 2 — Amber Alert (Score 500–580 | EWS > 0.65)

Triggers: 2× consecutive partial payments | CF volatility Low→High | LTI up >30%
Response: Personal call within 5 days | Restructuring offer | AA income refresh
Lead Time: 30–60 days | EWS Precision: ~72%

🔴 Tier 3 — Red Alert (Score <500 | DPD-30 | EWS > 0.85)

Triggers: DPD ≥ 30 | Payment <50% EMI × 2 months | Balance drop >60% | PD >55%
Response: Recoveries escalation 48h | Legal notice prep | Field visit >₹2L | NPA trigger
Lead Time: 0–30 days | EWS Precision: ~89%
